Going Somewhere Fun?

Fortify your adventures

Got Lots of Gear?

Outdoor activities like cycling, kayaking or hiking need
a lot of gear to make the strip safe and memorable. We
make packing smarter so you can play harder.

Ever Forget Something?

Unfortunately, remembering what you need is a lot more difficult
than you think. One missing item can ruin an entire adventure.

Introducing Viking App

We have figured out a way to solve your packing problems for good. We curate
and provide your packing list for you based on where you are going and what you
are doing. To make it easier for you we break down everything you do into three
types of trips/activities for you based on type of activity, temperature, and duration.
RAIDS, JOURNEYS AND EXPEDITIONS.

RAID

Short regular daily activities such
as hitting the Gym, morning surf
session, and your morning run.

JOURNEY

Any day trips that don't require
an overnight stay such as a hike,
snowboarding, or kayaking.

EXPEDITION

Longer overnight style trips
such as backpacking, climbing,
ski destination getaways, etc.
